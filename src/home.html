Trending Dishes

Injera with Doro Wat (Eritrean)

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup teff flour
  2. 2 cups water
  3. 1 chicken (for Doro Wat)
  4. Berbere spice mix

Steps:

  1. Prepare the injera batter and let it ferment.
  2. Cook the Doro Wat with spices and chicken.
  3. Serve the Doro Wat on Injera.

Spaghetti Carbonara (Italian)

Ingredients:

  1. Spaghetti
  2. Eggs
  3. Pancetta or guanciale
  4. Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta or guanciale.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese, then combine with pasta.

T'ej (Eritrean Honey Wine)

Ingredients:

  1. Honey
  2. Water
  3. Gesho leaves
  4. Yeast

Steps:

  1. Mix honey and water in a container.
  2. Add yeast and gesho leaves to ferment.
  3. Let it ferment for several weeks.

Margherita Pizza (Italian)

Ingredients:

  1. Pizza dough
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. Fresh mozzarella
  4. Fresh basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Prepare the pizza dough and spread tomato sauce.
  2. Add mozzarella and basil leaves.
  3. Bake in a hot oven until crispy.
